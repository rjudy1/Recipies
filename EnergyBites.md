### Energy Bites

##### Ingredients
- 1 cup rolled oats
- 2/3 cup peanut butter
- 1/2 cup mini chocolate chips
- 1/3 cup honey
- 1 tsp vanilla extract

##### Steps
1. Mix all ingredients.
2. Refridgerate 2 hours.
3. Roll into balls and store in fridge.
